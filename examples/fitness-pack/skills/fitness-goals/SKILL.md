# Fitness Goals

Set, track, and achieve your fitness goals with intelligent progress monitoring.

## Features
- Set SMART fitness goals (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound)
- Track daily, weekly, and monthly progress
- Get motivational reminders and check-ins
- Adjust goals based on progress and life changes

## Goal Types Supported
- Weight loss/gain targets
- Strength goals (lift specific weights)
- Endurance goals (run distance/time)
- Consistency goals (workout frequency)
- Body composition goals
- Health metrics (blood pressure, heart rate, etc.)

## Usage
- "Set a goal to lose 10 pounds in 3 months"
- "I want to bench press 200lbs by December"
- "Goal: run a 5K in under 25 minutes"
- "Show my goal progress"
