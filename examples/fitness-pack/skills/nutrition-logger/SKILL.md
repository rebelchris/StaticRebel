# Nutrition Logger

Track your daily nutrition intake and monitor eating habits.

## Features
- Log meals, snacks, and beverages
- Track calories, macros, and micronutrients
- Monitor eating patterns and timing
- Set nutrition goals and targets
- Food database with common items

## Usage
- "Log breakfast: oatmeal with banana"
- "I ate a chicken salad for lunch"
- "Track 2 glasses of water"
- "Show my nutrition for today"
- "What's my calorie count?"
