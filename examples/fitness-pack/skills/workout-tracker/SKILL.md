# Workout Tracker

Track your workout sessions, exercises, and progress over time.

## Features
- Log workout sessions with exercises, sets, reps, and weights
- Track workout duration and calories burned
- View workout history and progress
- Set personal records and achievements

## Usage
Simply mention any workout-related activity and I'll help you log it!

Examples:
- "I just did 3 sets of 10 push-ups"
- "Finished a 30-minute run"
- "Bench press 135lbs for 5 reps"
- "Show my workout history"

## Data Tracked
- Exercise type and muscle groups
- Sets, reps, and weight/resistance
- Workout duration
- Date and time
- Notes and feelings about the workout
